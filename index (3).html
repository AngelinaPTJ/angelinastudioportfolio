About

Image-maker.
Story-teller.

I'm a visual communication student. My persona focuses on the identity or idea of my projects and turning them into a solution of humanity. Compelling stories usually inspire me the most.

Like Walter Wanderley's song, I design for the 'Blue Lady.' My work starts where I identify stories between users and technology. I specialise in restorative design that 'chases the blues away,' turning frustrated touchpoints into moments of reconciliation.

Currently based in Surabaya, Indonesia, open to remote collaborations worldwide.

Process

01
Research & Immersion

Every project starts with deep listening. I study the context, the culture, the mood — before a single mark is made.

Sketching, moodboarding, and unexpected references. I look for the visual truth that others might overlook.

Precision in execution. Every curve, weight, and tone is considered until the work feels inevitable.
